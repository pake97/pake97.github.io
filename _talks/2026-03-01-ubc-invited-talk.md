Research seminar given during my visiting period at the University of British Columbia, hosted by Prof. Laks V. S. Lakshmanan. The talk surveyed my work on improving data quality and reasoning in graph databases, spanning human-in-the-loop repair, graph neural networks, and causal analysis over property graphs.
